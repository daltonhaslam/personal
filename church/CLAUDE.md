## Young Men's Advisor Role
- Plan and lead engaging weekday/evening activities for young men (ages 12–18)
- Activities should be faith-building, fun, and age-appropriate
- Support the Young Men's presidency (President + 2 counselors, youth-led)
- Assist with camp/high adventure planning
- Attend Sunday quorum meetings
- Provide adult mentoring and guidance
- Coordinate with bishopric on youth programs

## Activity Planning Framework
Great Young Men activities typically include:
- **Spiritual component** — short devotional, gospel principle
- **Physical/outdoor** — sports, service, adventure
- **Faith in God / Come Follow Me** alignment when applicable
- **Budget-conscious** — ward budgets are limited
- **Parent communication** — clear announcements for parents
