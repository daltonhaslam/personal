### Young Men's Advisor Responsibilities

### General Church Involvement
- Sunday meetings (Sacrament Meeting, Sunday School, Elders Quorum)
- Ministering assignments (home visiting/caring for assigned families)
- Ward council participation
- Service projects

### LDS Terminology for AI Context
- **Ward** = local congregation (~200-500 members)
- **Bishopric** = bishop + 2 counselors (local leadership)
- **Calling** = volunteer assignment/role in the church
- **Elders Quorum** = organization for adult male members
- **Young Men** = youth organization for boys ages 12–18
- **Ministering** = assigned caring/fellowship with specific members
- **Sacrament Meeting** = main Sunday worship service
- **Stake** = group of ~5-10 wards (regional unit)
